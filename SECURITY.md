# Security Policy

## Supported Versions

Only versions 1.x and higher are supported. Versions 0.x are considered unstable.

## Reporting a Vulnerability

Please report security vulnerabilities by filling out [the form on GitHub](https://docs.github.com/en/code-security/how-tos/report-and-fix-vulnerabilities/privately-reporting-a-security-vulnerability).

See: 🔗 [Privately reporting a security vulnerability](https://docs.github.com/en/code-security/how-tos/report-and-fix-vulnerabilities/privately-reporting-a-security-vulnerability)

In short:
1. On GitHub, navigate to the main page of the repository
2. Under the repository name, [click the _Security and quality_ tab](https://github.com/TypedSoftware/templatefile/security)
3. Click _Report a vulnerability_ to open the advisory form
4. Fill in the advisory details form
5. At the bottom of the form, click _Submit report_

Normally, your report will be acknowledged within 5 days and you will receive a more detailed response to your report within 10 days. These timelines are best-effort.

Thiss project does not have a bug bounty program.

Security vulnerabilities in dependencies should be reported to their respective maintainers.

## Disclosure Policy

Disclosure is handled on a case-by-case basis depending on the severity of the reported vulnerability.
